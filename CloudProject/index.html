    Announcements

    The Publicly Available Content Database was opened for trial access until 31.12.2022. The Publicly Available Content Database is designed to complement other databases and collections. It aggregates publicly available scientific content from a number of different sources around the world or provides links to full texts. It includes journal articles, preprints, books, conference proceedings and reports.

    Approximately 41,000 e-books with the following topics published by Elsevier are now available in full text. Focus: Agricultural, Biological, and Food Sciences Focus: Biomedical Engineering Focus: Biochemistry, Genetics and Molecular Biology Focus: Biomedical Science and Medicine Focus: Chemical Engineering Focus: Chemistry Focus: Energy Focus: Engineering Focus: Earth and Planetary Sciences Focus: Environmental Science Focus: Finance Focus: Forensics, Security, and Criminal Justice Focus: Immunology and Microbiology Focus: Mathematical Sciences Focus: Materials Science Focus: Neuroscience Focus: Psychology Focus: Pharmacology, Toxicology and Pharmaceutical Science Focus: Social Sciences

    Figure Reporter database is available for trial access until 11.12.2022. Figure Reporter is a database containing millions of academic images and these images can be searched. Figure Reporter extracts and indexes images and figures that are very important in the use of scientific knowledge from academic publications.
